Custom Software Development Services

We design and build tailored web applications, mobile apps, and digital products that help businesses in East Africa grow.

Saharasoft Solutions LTD is a software development company focused on turning business ideas into reliable, scalable digital products. From early-stage startups to established enterprises, we partner with teams across East Africa to deliver software that fits real workflows.

What We Build

Custom Web Application Development

We build responsive web applications using modern backend and frontend technologies. Whether you need an internal operations tool, a customer portal, or a SaaS platform, our team designs architecture that can grow with your user base.

Mobile App Development

Our Flutter and native mobile development teams create iOS and Android apps that work smoothly across devices. We focus on performance, offline usability, and clean user experiences for field teams, customers, and internal staff.

Product Design and UX

Great software starts with clear design. We run user research, wireframing, and prototyping sessions to make sure the final product is intuitive and aligned with business goals.

System Integration and APIs

We connect disparate systems — payment gateways, accounting tools, CRMs, SMS services, and more — through secure APIs and automated workflows.

Industries We Serve

We have delivered solutions for water utilities, real estate, hospitality, finance, logistics, and healthcare. Our domain experience helps us understand regulatory, operational, and customer-facing requirements quickly.

Our Development Process

  1. Discovery — We learn your business, users, and constraints.
  2. Design — We create wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes.
  3. Development — We build in iterative sprints with regular demos.
  4. Testing — We test for functionality, security, and performance.
  5. Deployment — We ship to production and provide ongoing support.
