Case study

TallyDue cut water-bill collection time from weeks to days

Client: A water utility in Kenya

Challenge: The utility was tracking meter readings, balances, and payments across disconnected spreadsheets and paper receipts. Monthly billing took weeks, follow-ups were manual, and revenue leakage was hard to trace.

Outcome: We replaced the process with TallyDue, a billing and customer management platform that imports readings, generates bills, tracks payments, and sends automated reminders. The utility now closes billing cycles in days and can see outstanding balances by zone, route, or individual account.
