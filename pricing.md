# Saharasoft Solutions — Pricing & Service Model

Saharasoft Solutions offers custom software development and product engineering services for African businesses. Specific pricing is tailored to each project. Contact us for a custom quote.

## Service Tiers

### 1. Discovery & Consultation

- **scope**: Requirements analysis, technical feasibility, architecture recommendations, and project roadmap.
- **deliverables**: Discovery report, proposed tech stack, effort estimate, and implementation plan.
- **pricing**: Fixed engagement fee based on project complexity and discovery depth.

### 2. Custom Project

- **scope**: End-to-end design and development of a web application, mobile application, or business operations system.
- **deliverables**: UX/UI design, working software, testing, deployment, and launch support.
- **pricing**: Project-based fee, typically milestone-based, determined by scope, timeline, and team requirements.

### 3. Retainer & Ongoing Support

- **scope**: Continuous development, maintenance, hosting support, bug fixes, and feature improvements after launch.
- **deliverables**: Monthly support hours, prioritized backlog, uptime monitoring, and regular updates.
- **pricing**: Monthly retainer based on required support level and response times.
